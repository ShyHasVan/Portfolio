Google Advanced Data Analytics Capstone

This project analyzed HR data for Salifort Motors to identify the key drivers of employee churn. The primary goal was to build a predictive model that could identify at-risk employees, allowing HR to implement proactive retention strategies.

Key Activities:

  • Built and evaluated a Random Forest Classifier, achieving 98.6% accuracy in predicting employee attrition.
  • Identified satisfaction level, tenure, and number of projects as the most critical factors influencing churn.
  • Discovered three distinct profiles of departing employees: the under-appreciated, the overworked, and the under-engaged.
  • Provided actionable recommendations, including deploying the model for risk scoring and implementing targeted career development programs.
